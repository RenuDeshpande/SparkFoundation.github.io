Welcome !

Our mission is to inspire students, let them integrate and help them innovate to build a better humankind.

The Sparks Foundation is a charitable organization running in India and Singapore, and expanding.

The Sparks Foundation is a startup. We started with basic charity endeavors under different names in the education sector. About a year ago, we launched a full spectrum of our operations under the formal name of The Sparks Foundation.

At The Sparks Foundation, you will become a part of an elite league of world-class universities alumni and industry experts with creative minds. You’ll be in company of like minded people with a single goal of helping students and improving education worldwide.
